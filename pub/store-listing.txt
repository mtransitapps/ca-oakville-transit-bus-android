Full description:
This application adds Oakville Transit buses information to MonTransit BETA.

This app contains the buses schedule and the latest news from www.oakvilletransit.ca and @oakvilletransit on Twitter.

BETA: Thanks for your patience while the last features are being added and the last bugs are being fixed.

Oakville Transit serves Oakville in Ontario, Canada.

Once this application is installed, the MonTransit BETA app will display buses information (schedule...).

This application only has a temporary icon and no screen: download the MonTransit BETA app (free) in the "More ..." section bellow or by following this Google Play link http://goo.gl/pCk5mV

You can install this application on the SD card but it is not recommended.

The information comes from the GTFS file provided by Oakville Transit and Town of Oakville.
http://www.oakville.ca/data/oakville-transit-route-information.html

This application is free and open-source:
https://github.com/mtransitapps/ca-oakville-transit-bus-android

This app is not related with Oakville Transit and Town of Oakville.

Permissions:
- Other: required to read news from www.oakvilletransit.ca and Twitter.
